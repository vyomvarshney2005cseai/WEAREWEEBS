Driving 3.2M Concurrent Viewers for a Virtual Concert Launch

When a top-tier VTuber agency needed to maximize live viewership for their flagship virtual concert event, they turned to We Are Weebs. Our team deployed a 21-day programmatic blitz across YouTube pre-roll, Twitch display, Twitter/X promoted posts, and premium anime publisher inventory.

Using our proprietary audience graph, we identified and activated 14 high-value fan segments across 8 markets, achieving an unprecedented 8.4% click-through rate—more than 6× the industry average for entertainment campaigns. The campaign generated 47 million impressions at a cost-per-completed-view of just $0.03, culminating in a record-breaking 3.2 million simultaneous viewers on concert night.

300% ROAS on a Gacha Game Global Launch

A leading Japanese game publisher partnered with We Are Weebs to orchestrate the global user acquisition strategy for their flagship gacha RPG. The mandate: acquire 1 million high-LTV players across NA, EU, and SEA within the first 30 days of launch—at a target CPI under $2.00.

We deployed a multi-format programmatic strategy combining playable ads, rewarded video, and native placements across 18 ad networks and DSPs. Our payer-lookalike models, trained on first-party data from similar titles, enabled us to pinpoint high-value players at scale. The result: 1.4 million installs at a blended CPI of $1.18, with D30 ROAS hitting 300%—exceeding the publisher's target by 50%. Day-7 retention outperformed benchmarks by 40%.
